Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. What data we collect

When you post a gig, we collect: gig title, description, budget, your handle/company name, contact info (Telegram/X/Email) and date.

2. How we use it

We only display your gig publicly so people can apply. We never sell your data.

3. Stripe payments

All payments are processed by Stripe. They handle all card data and are fully GDPR compliant. We only receive confirmation that the payment succeeded.

4. Your rights

You can request deletion of your gig or data at any time. Just DM me on X @phoneonlygigs or email contact@phoneonlygigs.com

5. Data retention

Gigs stay online until you ask us to remove them. No user accounts yet.
